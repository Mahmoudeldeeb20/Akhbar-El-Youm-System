Our Story

Date of establishment of the Academy: Decision of the Ministry of Higher Education No. 53 of 1999 on 16/5/1999.The university is composed of the College, various graduate programs and interdisciplinary committees organized into four academic research divisions and seven professional sectors. Beyond the journalism and computer sciences, AYA is also well known for its professional sectors in engineering, which include the sector of Mechanic and electric and its branches, the sector Business Administration.

Projects Exhibition Department of Mechanical Engineering Academy

10:00 AM 02:00PM

The Akhbar Al-Yawm Academy held an exhibition for the various teams in the Department of Mechanical Engineering on Sunday, 29/4/2018 under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Perlanti Iskandar, head of the department, where she proposed the idea of a project exhibition and coordination between professors and students to develop ideas and implement them.

30 July

Scientific visit to the press department at Akhbar Al Youm Academy

10:00 AM 02:00PM

The Department of Journalism at the Academy organized a scientific and recreational visit to the city of Alexandria. The visit program included the Bibliotheca Alexandrina to identify the use of computer and technological innovations to enhance the library and communicate with its public as well as the organizational and administrative structure of the library within the framework of the digital communication and the economics of institutions. Dr. Mona Omran, head of the department.
